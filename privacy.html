Vimpact — Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • your login and password details, in connection with the User Account sign-in process;
  • details of any requests or transactions you make through the Services;
  • information about your activity on and interaction with Vimpact, such as your IP address and the type of device or browser you use;
  • communications between you and Vimpact (for example, when you ask for support, send us questions or comments, or report a problem) including service related e-mails from us; and
  • information that you submit to Vimpact in the form of comments, contributions to discussions, or messages to other Users.

Your Account Information and Profile

When you create an account, we create a basic profile page for you on Vimpact, containing your account name, the date the account was created, and a list of Challenge you have funded, created or initiated. Whenever your account name appears on Vimpact, people can click your account name to see your profile. Information that will be publicly viewable on your profile, or elsewhere on Vimpact include:

  • the account name, and the date it was created
  • any information you choose to add to your profile (like a picture, bio, or your location)
  • Challenge you have funded
  • Challenge you have created or self-initiated
  • any comments you’ve posted on Vimpact
  • if you have "Joined" a Challenge

Please note that each Challenge page displays the account name of the Challengee and lists the community of Users who have funded the Challenge and their account name will be posted.

  • send you marketing communications we think you may find useful, but only in accordance with your preferences;

  • administer your account with us;
  • enable us to contact you regarding any question you make through the Services;
  • develop and test new product and features

  • diagnose or fix technology problems

  • when you fund a Challenge, the Challengee will know your account name;
